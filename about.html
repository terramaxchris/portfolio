About the Artist

I'm a traditional and digital artist and I've been posting my art online for 20 years now across sites like DeviantArt, Instagram and AO3 under the name kokiri85. But these sites rise and fall, and they all have their flaws.

I've always wanted to make a proper portfolio site of my own, but never had the skills to build it myself. Now that's finally changing! Now as I build my programming skills, I can use this site to showcase both that and my art.
