Burnsville

Most of these are from the period AFTER I moved back from France to Minnesota, but before I got my apartment in the Twin Cities. I had gotten really used to talking long, interesting wlaks in France, and it was some ugly culture shock to realize how hard it is to walk anywhere in the suburbs. Mostly I just walked around Early Lake.

All images were drawn by me without use of AI. Bootstrap columns were used for responsive layout.
