Eagan, MN

I work in Eagan and have carefully arranged my life to make bussing work. This isn't always entirely convenient, but one of the things I like about it is how much more aware of the landscape I can be. I can take pictures out the bus window because I'm not driving, and the area arond my stop has a lot of open fields.

Drawn during Lent in 2024, from a photo I took while walking through some parking lots behind buildings. There's a scruffy wooded area back there, and I find the subtle colors in the brown grass interesting to try to capture.

This website is a portfolio for my personal art. All images were drawn by me, Kokiri85, witohut use of AI. JavaScript was used to make the photo gallery, and Bootstrap columns for responsive layout.
