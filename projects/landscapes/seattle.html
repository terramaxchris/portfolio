Seattle

I started drawing landscapes in Seattle some years before what's posted here, but that work is saved on a machine I don't currently have easy access to. These few were all drawn in 2019, in the late winter and early spring during a period when I was only partially employed and waiting on a job application to decide whether to stay or move back to Minnesota. I didn't get the job and decided to move, but during the wait I spent my time going to favorite spot sin the city to draw.

This website is a portfolio for my personal art. All images were drawn by me, Kokiri85, witohut use of AI. JavaScript was used to make the photo gallery, and Bootstrap columns for responsive layout.
