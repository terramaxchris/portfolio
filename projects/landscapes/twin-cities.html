Twin Cities, MN

After I moved back to Burnsville from France in 2021, I found a job on a busline, and then an apartment in St. Paul that was also on that bus line. Public transit is less convenient in the Twin Cities than it was in Seattle, and it's much less walkable than Blois France, but I am committed to a car-free lifestyle.

Being car-free means that I walk a lot, and wait for the bus a lot, and by this point I have thoroughly trained my brain to be constantly looking for cool landscape views.

Dated 2/25/2023, drawn from a photo I took when walking home. I found the way the streetlights lit up the branches of the tree really compelling, along with the way the city lights in the background seemed to mirror the few stars visible above. I also really like how the organic lines of trees liik against straight vertical lines of things like telephone poles or streetlights.

This website is a portfolio for my personal art. All images were drawn by me, Kokiri85, witohut use of AI. JavaScript was used to make the photo gallery, and Bootstrap columns for responsive layout.
